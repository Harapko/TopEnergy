Advantages

Cost effectiveness
Boilers that run on straw offer significant cost savings over traditional fuel sources. Straw is an abundant and inexpensive resource, making it an economical choice for heating.
Environmental Friendliness
Using straw as a fuel source significantly reduces carbon emissions compared to fossil fuels. It is a renewable resource that helps in decreasing the carbon footprint.
Efficiency
Boilers running on straw are highly efficient, providing consistent and reliable heat. They are designed to maximize energy output while minimizing waste.
